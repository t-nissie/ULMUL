ULMUL (ulmul.rb)

ULMUL (ulmul.rb) is a converter from Ultra Lightweight MarkUp Language to (X)HTML. It will convert TeX-like equations into MathML. Please see README-??.xhtml files in each language. You may need Firefox to browse XHTML files with MathML.
